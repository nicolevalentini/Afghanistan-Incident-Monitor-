Workflow
1. Fetch
Pulls up to 50 articles per source. Afghan outlets show all articles; Reuters/BBC/RFE/RL are filtered for Afghanistan mentions.
2. Review
Click ▼ Fill Data on each article, read it, then fill in location, province, attack type, perpetrator, target, and casualties.
3. Verify
Click Mark Verified once confirmed. Click ✕ to dismiss irrelevant articles (you can restore them anytime).
4. Export
Click Export CSV at the top — downloads a UTF-8 file ready for Excel with all your structured data pre-filled.
