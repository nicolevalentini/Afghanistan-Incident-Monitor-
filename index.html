How it works
1. Fetch
Pulls the latest articles from 20 RSS sources. Afghan outlets show all articles; Reuters/BBC/RFE/RL are filtered for Afghanistan mentions.
2. Review
Click ▼ Fill Data on each article, read it, then fill in location, province, attack type, perpetrator, target, and casualties.
3. Verify
Click Mark Verified to confirm a genuine incident. Click ✕ to dismiss irrelevant articles — you can restore them anytime.
4. Export
Click Export CSV in the top right to download an Excel-ready file with all your structured data.
